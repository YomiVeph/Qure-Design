Frequently asked questions

Q1: How do i join a hospital queue?

You can join a queue by creating an account, logging in, and selecting the hospital and department you wish to visit. Your position in the queue will be displayed in real time.

Q2: Can i track my queue status remotely?

Yes! After joining a queue, you can check your status through the app or website anytime, and receive updates when it's your turn.

Q3: What if i miss my turn?

If you miss your turn, you can notify the hospital staff via the Contact Us page or by calling them. They will advise you on the next available slot or reassign you in the queue.

Q4: Can I book appointments in advance?

Absolutely. You can book appointments for available slots in different departments, reducing your wait time.

Q5: How do I update my contact information?

Go to your profile in the app or website, and update your phone number or email. Make sure it's correct to receive real - time notifications

Q6: What should i do if the system isn't working?

Contact Customer Care via live chat, call, or email. You can also submit a support ticket through the app, and a staff member will assist you promptly.
